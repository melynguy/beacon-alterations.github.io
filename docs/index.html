Walk-in's are welcome!

Come drop in to simply say "Hello" or ask us questions. We know the experience can seem daunting, and that's why we are here to help!

With over 30 years of expertise in custom tailoring that first began at Nordstroms, our passion in high quality, professional and affordable services to reach customer satisfaction is what drives us.

Beacon Alterations accommodates a wide range of services from altering apparel, tailoring suits and wedding gowns, creating custom clothing and even mending anything from plush toys to camping tents! All of our services are provided for men, women and children.

We are located in Beacon Hill -- conveniently located 2-minute walk from the Beacon Hill Link Light Rail Station, 7-minute drive from Seattle's Stadiums, 12-minutes from Downtown, 16-minutes from Bellevue and 20-minutes from the University of Washington.
